A new office, a new chapter

Today, the doors were locked for the very last time at the old Inter Designs office. The next chapter in our story begins, and we're excited to where it will take us. We will miss the quirks of the office which made it home, from the dodgey door that never shut, to the leaky roof which caused ever so many problems

So what is next for us? Well we have a brand new office to call home! This is a huge step forward for the company, the extra space will allow us to expand our team for the foreseeable future. We would like to thank everyone reading this for your overwhelming support, we wouldn't be here without you.
