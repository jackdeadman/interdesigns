Shape Up!

The following game is only accessible to users whom have a mouse and keyword.

Test your mouse and keyboard skills in Shape Up! Press the corresponsing number key to change the shape of the cursor. Hover over a shape to collect it, collect all the shapes before the time runs out to progress to the next level. The faster you are the more points you will recieve!
